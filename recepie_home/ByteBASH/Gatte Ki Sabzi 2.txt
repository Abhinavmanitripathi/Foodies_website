Gatte Ki Sabzi is a traditional dish of Rajasthan. It is made from gram flour gatta and curd gravy.
Gattes are gram flour fritters which are steamed.
These are prepared from flour with gram flour, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and a little ghee.
To make Gatte ki Sabzi, first of all Gatte is made with a mixture of gram flour and spices. Then they are boiled in water. After this, they are cooked in spicy curd gravy.
